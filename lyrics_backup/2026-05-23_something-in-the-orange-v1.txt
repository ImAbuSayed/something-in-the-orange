[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Something in the orange of the fading sun,
Tells me that our evening has just begun.
Pulling you closer in the amber light,
I'm losing my senses to you tonight.

[Verse 1]
Dust on the dashboard, boots by the door,
The way that you're looking makes me want so much more.
Your hand on my shoulder, a spark in the dark,
Leaving a sensual trail to my heart.

[Pre-Chorus]
The shadows are dancing across the room,
Swept up in the scent of your subtle perfume.
I lean in a little, I'm drawing you near,
The hunger I have is becoming so clear.

[Chorus]
There's something in the orange, a fire in your eyes,
Burning right through my favorite disguise.
Tangled in the velvet of a midnight breeze,
You bring a restless cowboy to his knees.

[Post-Chorus]
Yeah, falling into you feels so right,
Lost in the glow of the orange light.

[Verse 2]
The record is spinning a slow-burning tune,
Under the pull of a honey-gold moon.
Tracing the curves of your beautiful frame,
Igniting a spark that turns into a flame.

[Pre-Chorus 2]
The shadows are dancing across the room,
Swept up in the scent of your subtle perfume.
I lean in a little, I'm drawing you near,
The hunger I have is becoming so clear.

[Chorus]
There's something in the orange, a fire in your eyes,
Burning right through my favorite disguise.
Tangled in the velvet of a midnight breeze,
You bring a restless cowboy to his knees.

[Post-Chorus 2]
Yeah, falling into you feels so right,
Lost in the glow of the orange light.

[Bridge]
The silence is loud in the candlelit room,
Our shadows are dancing inside of the gloom.
I'm caught in the echo of every slow breath,
A gentle surrender, a beautiful theft.
Say my name softly... Saeed tonight... [Whispered] (Saeed... Saeed...)
Two burning hearts in the midnight blue.

[Spoken]
Just let the world fade, it's only us here.
There is nowhere to go, and nothing to fear.

[Final Chorus]
There's something in the orange, a fire in your eyes,
Burning right through my favorite disguise.
Tangled in the velvet of a midnight breeze,
You bring a restless cowboy to his knees.
